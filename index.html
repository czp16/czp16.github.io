Zhepeng Cen zcen[at]andrew.cmu.edu

I am a final-year Ph.D. student in Safe AI Lab at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Ding Zhao. I obtained my bachelor's degree at the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. Prior to CMU, I had a wonderful time as a summer intern in USC INK Lab. My research lies at the intersection of reinforcement learning and optimization. I am particularly interested in the emerging challenges of applying RL to large foundation models and learning-based autonomy, with a focus on sparse signal and data efficiency.

I will be on the job market this year and looking for the full-time research position in industry!
